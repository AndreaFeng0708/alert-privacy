Delete Your ALERT Account

ALERT is a disaster preparedness and safety app developed by Andrea Feng. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and data.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to support@alertdisasters.com from the email address associated with your ALERT account
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your ALERT username in the email

We will process your request and confirm by email within a reasonable time.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (username, email, password)
  • Your quiz results and checklist/recovery progress
  • Your community posts and comments
  • Your Family Circle memberships and safety check-in history
  • Any incident reports you submitted

What may be retained

None — once a deletion request is confirmed, all of the data listed above is permanently removed from our database. We do not retain a backup copy beyond what's needed to complete the deletion process itself.
