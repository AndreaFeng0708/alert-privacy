Privacy Policy — ALERT

Last updated: July 2026

What We Collect

Account info: username, email address, and a securely hashed password.

Location (approximate): used only while the app is open, to show nearby shelters, nearby disaster alerts, and to attach a location to your "I'm Safe" check-ins or SOS alerts. Location is never collected in the background, and you can deny or revoke it at any time in your device settings.

Quiz & checklist progress: your quiz scores and preparedness checklist progress, stored against your account.

Community content: posts and comments you choose to submit are visible to other users.

How We Use It

  • To show you relevant disaster alerts and nearby shelters
  • To let your Family Circle see your safety check-in status and SOS alerts
  • To track your quiz/checklist progress across sessions

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not share your location with anyone outside your own Family Circle
  • We do not track your location in the background when the app is closed

Third-Party Data Sources

Disaster alerts are pulled from public government feeds (USGS, NOAA) and OpenStreetMap contributors for shelter data. These requests do not send your personal account information to those services.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your account data is retained while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us using the details below — we'll remove it within a reasonable time.

Security

Passwords are stored using industry-standard one-way hashing. Traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS).

Children's Privacy

ALERT is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or a data deletion request? Contact: support@alertdisasters.com
